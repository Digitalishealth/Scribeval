Endocrinology Outpatient Review
Patient: Mr Thompson
Referring GP: Dr Ali
Attending: Dr Nguyen (Endocrinology Registrar)

Medications:

Feet: Reduced 10g monofilament sensation bilaterally. Pedal pulses present, reduced on right. No ulceration, no deformity.

Assessment:
1. Poorly controlled T2DM (HbA1c 9.8%) with recurrent sulfonylurea-related hypoglycaemia.
2. Microvascular complications: retinopathy, peripheral neuropathy, likely diabetic nephropathy.
3. Suboptimal BP control (target <130/80 given diabetes + proteinuria).
4. High cardiovascular risk — family history of premature IHD, proteinuria, exertional dyspnoea, ex-smoker.

Plan:
Diabetes:
1. STOP gliclazide — unsafe given recurrent hypoglycaemia. Take today's dose as usual, cease from tomorrow morning to avoid rebound hyperglycaemia.
2. RESTART GLP-1 agonist (semaglutide) at low dose with slow titration. Counselled on nausea management and gradual dose escalation.
3. COMMENCE empagliflozin 10 mg daily — dual benefit for glycaemic and renal / cardiovascular outcomes. Counselled on genital mycotic infection and UTI risk.
4. If HbA1c remains >8% at 3 months, discuss basal insulin initiation.

Blood pressure:
5. ADD amlodipine 5 mg daily. Monitor for peripheral oedema. Target <130/80.

Investigations:
6. Repeat HbA1c in 3 months.
7. Urine ACR to quantify proteinuria.
8. eGFR, fasting lipids.

Referrals:
9. Diabetic foot clinic — education and detailed assessment.
10. Cardiology — given strong family history of premature IHD, proteinuria, and exertional dyspnoea. Letter to be sent from this clinic. To rule out ischaemic heart disease.

Safety-netting / Patient education:
11. Hypoglycaemia education: Symptoms (shakiness, sweating, confusion); treatment with 15g fast-acting carbohydrate; retest in 15 min and repeat if required; call ambulance if severe or unable to self-treat.
12. Household support: Wife to be educated on severe hypo recognition and management; educational video link to be shared.
13. Always carry glucometer.
14. Review in 6 weeks for medication tolerance and titration.
