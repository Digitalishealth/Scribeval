## Transcript

**GP:** Your blood test shows iron deficiency anaemia. Have you noticed black stools, rectal bleeding, weight loss, or abdominal pain?

**Patient:** I am tired. No black stools or bleeding that I have seen. I have lost about four kilos but I thought it was stress.

**GP:** Any heavy periods?

**Patient:** No, I had a hysterectomy years ago.

**GP:** Because you are 58, post-hysterectomy, and have weight loss, we need to look for gastrointestinal blood loss. I am referring you for colonoscopy and gastroscopy. Start oral iron. I will check repeat FBC/ferritin in four weeks and my team will track that the endoscopy appointment is booked.

**Patient:** What if I do not hear from them?

**GP:** Call us if no appointment within two weeks, or urgently for black stools, bleeding, chest pain, fainting, or worsening shortness of breath.
