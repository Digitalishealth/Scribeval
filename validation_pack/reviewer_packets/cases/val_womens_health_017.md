**GP:** You wanted to restart the combined pill. Any migraines, aura, blood clots, smoking, or high blood pressure?

**Patient:** I get migraines with zig-zag lights before the headache. I do not smoke. My mum had a clot after surgery.

**GP:** Any personal clot, stroke, chest pain, or current pregnancy possibility?

**Patient:** No personal clot or stroke. Pregnancy test yesterday was negative.

**GP:** Migraine with aura means the combined pill is not safe because of stroke risk. We can use a progestogen-only pill, implant, IUD, or injection.

**GP:** We will start that, discuss missed-pill rules, and review bleeding or side effects in three months. Seek urgent care for chest pain, leg swelling, severe new headache, weakness, or vision loss.
