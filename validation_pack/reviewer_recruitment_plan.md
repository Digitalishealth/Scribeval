# Reviewer Recruitment Plan

This recruitment plan supports reviewer sourcing and governance only. It is not validation evidence; validation claims require completed independent clinician ratings, reliability analysis, adjudicated consensus, and claim-readiness checks.

Benchmark unit: `whole transcript -> final note quality score`

## Recruitment Targets

- Primary/secondary reviewers: 2
- Adjudicators: 1
- Total qualified reviewers: 3
- Minimum years post registration: 1
- Registration status: `current`
- Conflict of interest: `none`
- Training completed required: True
